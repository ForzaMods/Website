XP with 999 Engine

You need to have enough credits to purchase 2019 BUGATI DIVIO

Once you purchase the car go to car mastery and wait before unlocking XP

Go to engine select type as float, truncated scan option and value "20000"

Getting the XP

You will find around 300 (count on top) addresses, hit next scan button 2-3 times.

Select all the addresses and exclude the green ones and input your desired XP.

Go back to game and now unlock XP your done. Enjoy!
