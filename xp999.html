XP with 999 Engine

Recommendations: car that has a 7000,9000,15000 xp skillpoint value, works the best

You can do this with any car you want

You need to have atleast one skillpoint

Start of the method

Go to car mastery and wait before unlocking XP

Go to 999 engine select type as float, truncated scan option and enter the value of the xp skillpoint

Getting the XP

You will find around 300-750 (count on top) addresses (if you didnt find that low dont worry), add .000 at the end of your value box and hit next scan button 4-5 times.

Select all the addresses and exclude the green ones (hold ctrl and click lmb on them) and input your desired XP. (when you dont see green just select all)

Go back to game and now unlock XP, and your done. Enjoy!
