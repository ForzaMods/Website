Wheelswap Tutorial

1. Go to \media\cars\_library\scene\wheels and choose any wheel you dont want anymore, in this case i use HAL_Indy and extract it to a new folder on your Desktop.

2. Choose the car that you want the wheels from which can be found in \media\cars, i picked mg_metro6r4_86. Open the zip, inside the zip go to \scene\_library\Scene\Wheels\ and extract MG_Metro6R4_86_wheelLR.modelbin to the new folder you created.

3. Copy the name of the wheel you extracted earlier (HAL_Indy_wheelLF), delete the file and rename MG_Metro6R4_86_wheelLR to HAL_Indy_wheelLF.

4. Inside the car zip go to \Textures\AO\ and extract MG_Metro6R4_86_wheelLR_AO.swatchbin to where the wheel texture is (FOLDER YOU CREATED\Textures\AO) and repeat step 3 again.

5. Create a new .zip called HAL_Indy and put both MG_Metro6R4_86_wheelLR_AO.modelbin + Texture folder inside of it.

6. Go to \media\stripped\mediaoverride\rc0 and create new folders that follow the folder structure of the wheels from earlier. So inside rc0 create a folder called cars, inside cars > _library, inside _library > scene and inside scene > wheels. Copy the modified wheel zip into the wheels folder.

(If you cant find the texture inside car zip try looking into the texture zips called Textures or Textures_pri_45 in \Cars_library. You should find them in \ao_wheels\swatches\ or \libraryao\wheels\)
