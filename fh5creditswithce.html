Credits With Draff Engine

1. Open spins until you get a car you already own

2. Check for how much you can sell the car (where it shows keep or sold for : ??.???CR)

3. Open Draff Engine, select Forza Horizon 5 then search for double the amount of CR you can sell the car

(For example if it shows "Sell for 35.000CR", search for "70000" in Cheat Engine)

4. After scanning, only select the white values, not the red and green ones (if there is some)

5. Then replace it by what you want

(note that you will have half the CR that you entered, if you replaced 70000 by 300000, you will receive 150000 when selling the car)
