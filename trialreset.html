Trial Reset for 3DSimED3/010 Editor

1. Type "regedit" into the Windows search bar and open Registry Editor.

2. Open "Computer\HKEY_CURRENT_USER\SOFTWARE"

3. Find "SimGarage" (3DSimED3) or "SweetScape" (010 Editor) folder.

4. Right click the folder and select "Delete".

5. Restart the computer and trial period will be reset.
