Clear tails

Names may differ for each car but layout is the same. This is using the names for the S13.

1. Open glassLTL and glassRTL modelbins.

2. Within the first few lines you will see "border_red" "border_white" "glass_red" "glass_white" "border_amber" "glass_amber" or something similar.

3. Directly after the term there will be 00,01,02,03,etc

4. Now find the position string (starts with 80 3F, will be 30 long including 80 3F and 4 00's at minimum)

5. Shortly before or after you will see FF FF XX 00 FF FF FF or similar.

6. The XX will be 00,01,02,etc and you will need to change it to what is desired.

7. For example glass_white is 01, glass_red is 02, and glass_amber is 03. If i want all clear I will replace all FF FF 02 FF FF FF and FF FF 03 FF FF FF with FF FF 01 FF FF FF.

8. This will make all glass_red and glass_amber use the glass_white from the reverse lights.
